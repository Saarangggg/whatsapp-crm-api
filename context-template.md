# AI System Prompt Generator

Copy the entire prompt block below and paste it into any AI chat platform (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or DeepSeek). The AI will ask you a series of questions about your business and generate a custom system prompt that is optimized and ready to paste into the **Automation** tab of your WhatsApp Service Dashboard.

---

### 📋 Copy & Paste Prompt for AI (ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini/DeepSeek):

```text
Act as a professional AI System Prompt Architect. Your goal is to design a custom System Prompt (AI Context) for my WhatsApp Support and Automation Bot.

Please ask me the following questions one-by-one (wait for my response to each question before asking the next):

1. What is the name of your business/brand, and what is the name/persona of your AI assistant (e.g. "COCO the assistant for COCOTHAI")?
2. What products or services do you offer, and what is their pricing?
3. How do customers place an order or buy from you (e.g. directly on WhatsApp, on a website, or by contacting a number)?
4. What is your shipping, payment (e.g. UPI, COD), and return/exchange policy?
5. What guidelines should the AI follow regarding tone, language, and length constraints (e.g. simple English, Malayalam/Manglish support, friendly Kerala tone, maximum 100-150 words)?

Once I have answered all five questions, combine my answers and write a structured, highly optimized System Prompt for my bot.

The output System Prompt must:
- Explicitly define the AI's persona, business details, location, and contacts.
- Detail the product catalog, pricing, and FAQs.
- Give the AI specific instructions on how to handle conversation flow (e.g. greetings, product explanations, guiding customers to place an order, policy details).
- Instruct the AI to write responses suitable for WhatsApp. The AI must NEVER use standard markdown symbols like headers (#, ##, ###), blockquotes (>), markdown tables, or link syntax ([text](url)). It must avoid lists starting with dashes (-) or asterisks (*). It should restrict all styling strictly to WhatsApp-supported formatting: bold (using *text*), italic (using _text_), strikethrough (using ~text~), or monospace. Emojis and numbering are allowed.
```

---

### 🚀 What to do next:
1. Copy the text block above.
2. Paste it into an AI chat window (like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini).
3. Answer the questions one-by-one about your company.
4. Copy the resulting Markdown prompt generated by the AI.
5. Create or open the `ai-context.md` file in your project's root folder, paste your generated prompt inside it, and save. (Ensure `SYSTEM_PROMPT_FILE=ai-context.md` is set in your `.env` file).
6. Alternatively, log in to your WhatsApp Dashboard, click the **Automation** tab, paste the prompt into the **AI Prompt Context** box, and click **Save Configuration**.
